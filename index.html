About Us

Welcome to Vrindavan

Vrindavan Misthanna Bhandar – Pure Veg Indian Food in Hetauda, Nepal

Experience the true taste of India at Vrindavan Misthanna Bhandar, Hetauda’s most loved pure vegetarian restaurant and sweet shop. Conveniently located on the way to Kathmandu, we serve a wide range of authentic Indian dishes, snacks, and traditional sweets prepared with love and purity. Whether you're traveling or a local food lover, our hygienic kitchen, warm hospitality, and 100% vegetarian menu make us the perfect stop for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Visit Vrindavan today for a delightful culinary journey in the heart of Hetauda, Nepal.
